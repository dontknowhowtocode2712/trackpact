✅ How tasks are scored
There are 10 tasks per day across 4 categories: Fitness, Work, Habits, Good for Life.
Each task completed = 1 point. Each task missed = 0 points. Maximum 10 points per day.
Tasks apply every day including Saturday and Sunday.
Your daily score = points earned ÷ 10 × 100. Example: 7 tasks = 7 pts = 70%.

🌟 Score colour logic
Your own score: green if above 50%, red if at 50% or below.
Other players' scores are always shown in blue.
🔒 Day locking
Each day locks at midnight GST.
You have a 24-hour grace window to log the previous day. After 24 hours it is permanently locked.
If no tasks are logged by midnight GST, that day is automatically scored 0 points.
📈 Weekly points & percentage
Weekly points = sum of all daily points Mon–Sun. Maximum 70 points.
Weekly % = Points earned ÷ (Days played × 10) × 100
Future days are excluded — on Wednesday your % is out of 30, not 70.
Example — Thursday (4 days played)
Mon 9 + Tue 7 + Wed 10 + Thu 8= 34 pts
Max possible (4 × 10)= 40 pts

🏆 Winner declaration
Winner = highest weekly points at midnight Sunday GST.
Declared on Monday morning when the app is opened.
Leaderboard resets every Monday for the new week.
⚖ Tie-breaking
2-way tie: pot split — each tied player +5 AED, third player −10 AED.
3-way tie: net zero — everyone gets their 10 AED back.
💰 The weekly bet
Every player stakes 10 AED per week. Total pot = 30 AED.
No money changes hands weekly. A running P/L tally is kept all year.
Final settlement happens once at end of year.

📅 Season P/L
Each player's running P/L shown in green (profit) or red (loss) on the leaderboard.

New players start at 0 AED. Pot adjusts to active player count.
📝 The pledge
Every player must sign the Integrity Pledge and set a 4-digit PIN before first access.
Only mark a task complete if you have genuinely and fully completed it.
Do not share your PIN with other players.
🚫 What counts as a breach
Marking a task complete when it was not genuinely done.
Using the 24-hour window to falsify past entries.
Attempting to access another player's account.
Penalty decided collectively by the other players — final and binding.
⚙ Admin
Admin can correct task completions only. No direct score overrides.
Any correction should be agreed by all players before being made.
QWhat if I forget to log on Monday?
You have until midnight Tuesday GST to go back and log Monday. After that it's locked at 0.
QWhat counts as completing a task?
You must have genuinely and fully completed it. Partial completion does not count.
QCan I check tasks in advance?
No. You can only log today or yesterday within the 24-hour window.
QWhat if a task is added mid-week?
New tasks take effect from the next day only. Today's scores are unaffected.
QI'm leading — am I guaranteed to win?
No. The winner is only decided at midnight Sunday GST. Anyone can overtake you.
QWhen does money get paid?
No money changes hands weekly. One final settlement at end of year based on season P/L.
QWhat if I open the app on a new device?
Pick your name and enter your 4-digit PIN. Your PIN verifies your identity.
